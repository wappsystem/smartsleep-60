Welcome to SmartSleep Analyzer – Over 60 Validation.

You are invited to take part in remote research study to help us validate a sleep questionnaire tool called ‘SmartSleep Analyzer.’ This tool was developed in collaboration with sleep physicians and specialists to provide you with personalised feedback and recommendations to help you take charge of your sleep.

This study is for individuals with sleep complaints who are 60 years or over and who meet the criteria of:

- Shift-workers

- Short sleepers

- Delayed sleep phase disorder

To determine if you are eligible to participate, you will be asked to complete a short series of questions (maximum 5 minutes). All eligible participants will receive access to the SmartSleep Analyzer, and if applicable, will attend a 20-minute tele-consultation with a sleep physician/psychologist.

  

Your participation in this study is voluntary. If you are interested in participating, please proceed to the screening questionnaires
